Moreover, the argument emphasizes Poland's increasing engagement in international trade and its proactive participation in global discussions, which positions it as an emerging power ready for the G20. The convergence of these factors—strong economic performance, a favorable logistical position, investment in innovation, and international engagement—makes a compelling argument for Poland's potential inclusion in the G20.
